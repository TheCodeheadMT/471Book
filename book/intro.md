# Comp Sci 471.  Artificial Intelligence

  Introduction to major subjects and research areas in artificial intelligence (AI).  Topics include:  problem solving techniques, knowledge representation, machine learning, heuristic programming, AI languages, expert systems, natural language understanding, computer vision, pattern recognition, robotics, and societal impacts.  
  
  The course will also explore current and projected uses of AI in the Air Force.  Final exam or final project.  
  
  **Prereq:** Comp Sci 210 or Comp Sci 212. Sem hrs: 3 Fall.
